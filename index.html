Crossing Conundrum

A farmer who wishes to cross a river.He has with him: a small boat, a goat, a wolf and a pumpkin.
The boat can only take the farmer + ONE of his possessions per trip.

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Help the farmer get all 3 possessions on the South Bank.
2. Don't leave wolf & goat OR goat & pumpkin alone together at any time.
3. Boat will sink after 7 trips, so complete the task in under 7 trips.
